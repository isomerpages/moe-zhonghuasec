<h4>Student Leadership Programme</h4>
<p>Our Student Leadership Programme aims to nurture students to become dynamic
servant leaders committed to leading themselves and others. Customised
and run by our student leaders, we believe in the approach of “leaders
growing leaders”, empowering our student leaders in the training and mentoring
of their juniors.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Student Leadership Framework" src="/images/SLD_Framework.jpg">
</div>
<h5>The Five Practices of Exemplary Leadership</h5>
<p>Our school adopts the Kouzes and Posner’s Five Practices of Exemplary
Leadership Model in our Student Leadership Programme. Its principles are
based on a set of observable skills and abilities that drive behaviours.
Our student leaders are encouraged to practise these five exemplary leadership
practices when discharging their duties.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/5_practices.jpg">
</div>

<h5>Student Leadership Boards&nbsp;</h5>
<p><strong>(1) Class Care Committee</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Comprises the 6 student leaders managing each class (1 Chairperson, 3
Vice-Chairpersons and 2 Peer Support Leaders)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To support the form teachers in creating a positive class spirit, ensuring
a conducive learning environment, promoting CCE outcomes and Eco Stewardship
Programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To build a peer support culture which contributes to a caring and enabling
school environment</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>(2) CCA Leaders Board</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Comprises the CCA Head leading each CCA</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To lead and organise CCA activities and School Events (e.g. CCA Open House)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To motivate and develop CCA members to achieve positive learning outcomes</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>(3) Sports Leaders Board</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Comprises the 2 sports leaders elected from each class</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To lead and organise class and school sports activities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To promote an active and healthy lifestyle to the student body</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>(4) Student Council</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Comprises student councillors elected by the staff and student body</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To build a sense of belonging and school pride&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To strive to be a representative voice for the student body through active
engagement</p>
</li>
</ul>
