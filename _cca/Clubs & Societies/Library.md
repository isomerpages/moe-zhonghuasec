<p>As an additional CCA, the Library Club of Zhonghua Secondary School seeks
to develop servant leaders with effective leadership, organisational and
communication skills. In rendering service to the school community, student
librarians contribute to the curation of the library collection, and the
cultivation of a valuable learning and community space. The student librarians
are passionate about fostering a positive reading culture among Zhonghuarians.
Participation in the Library Club develops leadership, organisational,
and communication skills and confidence in our student librarians.</p>
