<p>Zhonghua's Drama Club aims for students to discover the power of empathy
through the art of drama. Drama Club members are bound by their love of
the Arts, and are vibrant individuals keen on expressing themselves on
stage.</p>
<p>In the Drama Club, students learn essential drama skills and vocal and
physical expressions to convey emotions, as well as techniques of production.
The Club aims to develop our members not just in performance but also in
becoming well-rounded and confident individuals.</p>
