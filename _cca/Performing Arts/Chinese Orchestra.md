<p>Zhonghua Chinese Orchestra seeks to provide a platform for young and interested
musicians to learn and share their passion for traditional Chinese music.
Although most of our new members enter with little or no prior musical
background, the nurturing environment in our orchestra helps our members
to grow in confidence and to develop an appreciation for Chinese music
and culture.</p>
<p>Throughout their journey with the orchestra, members learn personal responsibility,
teamwork, and resilience through a myriad of programmes that extend beyond
music. We also aim to nurture leadership competencies in our students by
offering members opportunities to lead and guide their peers in their roles
as sectional leaders or student conductors.</p>
<p>The Chinese Orchestra performs in numerous school-based events and celebrations.
The orchestra also puts up public performances and has performed consistently
well in major music competitions since its inception. These performances
are valuable opportunities for our students to develop a spirit of excellence
and to share the joy of Chinese music with others.</p>
