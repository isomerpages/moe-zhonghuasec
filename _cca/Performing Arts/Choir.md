<p>The Zhonghua Secondary School Choir comprises of young and enthusiastic
singers who are all passionate about music and singing. Our choristers
sing a diverse repertoire, ranging from soundtracks of well-known musicals
to songs from different languages and genres. The Choir is a valuable platform
for students to express themselves confidently and develop musicianship.</p>
<p><strong>Key Programmes:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Chorale Singing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Music theory lessons</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Performances at school events and celebrations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Performances at community and national events</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Choir Exchange Programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Team bonding activities</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Recent Achievements:</strong>
</p>

</li>
<li>
<p>Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation 2023 - Certificate of Accomplishment</p>
</li>
