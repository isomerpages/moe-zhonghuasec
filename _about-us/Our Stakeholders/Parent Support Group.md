<h4><strong>Welcome!</strong></h4>
<p>Zhonghua Secondary School Parent Support Group was set up in September
2001 with the blessing of the school.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/PSG_2023.jpg">
</div>
<p><strong> PSG organising Traditional Games on Racial Harmony cum Youth Day Celebrations</strong>
</p>
<h4><strong>Vision</strong></h4>
<p>School and parents working in partnership to support our students’ learning
and well-being.</p>
<h4><strong>Objectives</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To provide a supportive network for parents</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To work in partnership with school to enhance learning experiences for
students</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To be a bridge between parents and school by providing feedback and facilitating
the school’s communication to parents</p>
</li>
</ul>
