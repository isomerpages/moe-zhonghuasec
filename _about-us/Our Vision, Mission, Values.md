#### **Vision**

They will strive for excellence in every endeavour and will be noted for their wisdom and competence. Dignified and edified, and with enhanced social-emotional competencies, they will be confident remarkable leaders in specific field of specialisation who bring people together and evoke pride in being part of the team.
