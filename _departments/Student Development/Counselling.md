<p>Counselling is a collaborative relationship which provides the student
a listening ear and safe space – to explore issues &amp; solutions, develop
coping skills, facilitate positive change and improve mental well-being.</p>
<p>Students can request for a counselling appointment with the School Counsellor
/ Teacher Counsellors. Staff / Parents can also refer student / child for
school counselling support.</p>
<p><strong>Venue</strong>
<br>Counselling Room (at B2-06 – behind the School Hall)</p>
