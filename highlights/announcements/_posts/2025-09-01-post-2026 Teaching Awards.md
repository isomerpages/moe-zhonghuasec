<p>Dear parents, students, and alumni,</p>
<p>We invite you to nominate deserving Zhonghua Secondary School teachers
for the following two awards:
<br>
</p>
<p>1) The <strong>Arif Budiman Malay Language Teacher Award (AGAB)</strong>
<br>Jointly organised by the Malay Language Council, the Malay Language Teachers
Association, Berita Harian and the Malay Language Learning and Promotion
Committee, the award recognises outstanding Malay Language teachers in
primary and secondary schools, junior colleges and the Millennia Institute
who have made exceptional contributions to the teaching of the Malay Language.
<br>
<br>Nominations can be made online, from 2 Feb to 16 Mar 2026, via <a href="https://go.gov.sg/agab-2026" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/agab-2026</a>
</p>
<p></p>
